Writer and Podcast Host

I'm a writer and podcast host living in Los Angeles. My writing appears regularly in Slate, The Athletic, The Ringer, and a wide range of other publications. I am co-founder and co-host of Split Zone Duo, and I co-host Slate's sports program, Hang Up and Listen.
